About the Project

Dynamic Causal Modeling is a Bayesian statistical technique for reverse engineering time series data. One of the ongoing challenges in applying such statistical models is how to visualise the multiverse of possible outcomes that the algorithm derives. This site contains evidence-based visualisations demonstrating the multiverse of outcomes produced by DCM in climate modelling and physics simulation.
